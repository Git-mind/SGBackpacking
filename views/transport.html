MRT Information

Tourist EZ link

The Singapore Tourist Pass is a special ez-link card that offers tourist unlimited public transport travel on Singapore’s basic bus services, MRT and LRT trains for the duration that it is valid. You can now take in the sights and sounds of Singapore in the comfort of the island’s extensive train system and public bus network!

Things to note when on the train!

There are seats which are reserved for the Elderly, People of disabilities, Pregnant Women, People who are injured, etc... If you happen to be in this seat, we encourage you to give up the seat for these group of people 😀


Find out more >

1


When boarding and alighting the train, please mind the gap between the platform edge and the train.

2


Please move to the centre of the train car upon boarding to facilitate commuter traffic in and out of the train.

3


Do not lean against the train doors or the door to the train captain’s cabin which is located at both ends of the train.

4


Do not bring flammable liquids and gases onboard the train.

5


Please keep prams/strollers, bags and luggage out of the way of fellow commuters. These must never block walkways and doorways.

6


If you are caught between train doors, remain calm and alert other commuters to press the emergency communication button to inform the train captain. The train will not depart if its doors are not fully closed.

7


Do not eat or drink in the MRT.

8


Do not smoke inside the MRT.

9

Bus Information

Bus Fare Payment

You can pay for your bus fare using an adult stored value smartcard (EZ-link / Nets FlashPay) or the Singapore Tourist Pass (STP). You can also opt to pay in cash, but make sure you have the exact amount in change.keep an eye out for the electronic card reader — located next to the bus driver—and tap as you board.

Things to note when on the BUS!

The bus driver may not stop at every stop unless the driver is notified by the passenger. You may do so by pressing the bell on the handrails to alert the driver before the bus reaches the next stop.
